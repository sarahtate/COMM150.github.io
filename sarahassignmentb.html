Junior Player of The Month - Joey Tate

Joey Tate, 17, of Elm City, NC is the fist sibling among 12 to benefit from the recent and substancial growth of junior player competition, exemlified by the Junior International Championship series of tournaments, now in their second year.

Tate, in fact, welcomes the idea of having rivals. It is, he believes an important component of growth, not just in pool, but in any endeavor where people strive to be the best.
